## Information We Collect

We collect information to provide, secure, and improve our services. The categories below cover the typical types of data gathered by modern applications and online platforms:

| Category | Examples | Purpose |
|---------|----------|---------|
| **Account Information** | Email, profile name, authentication tokens | Account creation, login, personalization |
| **Usage Data** | In-app actions, feature interactions, navigation behavior | Feature improvement, analytics, debugging |
| **Technical & Device Data** | Device type, OS version, browser details, unique identifiers | Performance optimization, troubleshooting |
| **Network Data** | IP address, approximate location, network provider | Security, fraud prevention, load balancing |
| **Optional or User-Provided Data** | Preferences, notes, saved content, uploaded files | Personalization, voluntary feature use |
| **Communication Data** | Support messages, feedback, survey responses | Customer service and product enhancement |

We do not intentionally collect sensitive information—such as biometric identifiers, health data, or government-issued numbers—unless required for a specific feature and explicitly permitted by you and applicable law.
